Careers

Our mission at Valence is to help our customers “build potential”. Potential is what is possible. Potential is what anything can become. It is the energy between where you are today and where you can be in the future.

Our vision is that all companies in all industries will be able to harness the power of disruptive technologies and apply them to their own business for their own success – regardless if they are interested in cost savings, revenue growth, customer experience or employee advancement. We hope that – along with us – they will be able to create their own potential. But we need great people to make this happen!

And it isn’t just customers: we want you to build potential in your life, in your work and in your daily experiences. We want you to build your potential. It takes hard work, but that is part of the fun – hard work enables hard play. We take time to celebrate our success and make sure that Valence is more than just a “job”.

And of course, we offer a full range of benefits: 100% paid medical benefits, 401(k) matching, flexible work schedules, training, and numerous company and team building events. Valence is a dynamic place to build your career, innovate and learn. Come join us – let’s build potential together!

At our foundation, we are a business about relationships and customer success.
